Cinnamon Scrolls

Preparation Time: 25 minutes / Cooking Time: 12 Minutes / Makes: 12

Ingredients for dough:

  • 250g Self-raising flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 90g unsalted butter
  • 170ml milk

Ingredients for filling:

  • 60g unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons soft brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinammon

Icing

  • 60g Icing Confectioners
  • 2 teaspoons boiling water

1. Preheat the oven to 210C! Lightly grease a baking tray with butter/or line it with baking paper. Sift the flour and salt into a mixing bowl. Using fingertips, rub the butter into the flour. Make a well in the centre and add almost all the milk. Mix lightly with a flat bladed knife, until a soft dough is formed, adding more liquid if necessary. Knead the dough briefly on a lightly floured surface until smooth. Roll out to a 40 x 25 cm rectangle of 5mm thickness.

Next - Cinnamon Filling! - Lona, 2024

2. To make the filling, place all the ingredients in a mixing bowl. Beat mixture until light and fluffy using electric beater/ mixer. Spread the filling evenly over the dough rectangle and roll up from the long side. Using a sharp Knife, slice the dough into twelve 3cm pieces. Place these pieces close to one-another, cut side up on the prepared tray. Bake for 12 minutwa, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and leave to cool

3. To make the icing, combine the sifted icing sugar and boiling water in a small mixing bowl until well combined, then drizzle over your cinammon scrolls and its ready to eat!
